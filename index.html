FAQ

Questions before you step inside?

Here’s how Thinks. stays private, adaptive, and restorative.

Is Thinks. a social network?

No feeds, no algorithmic nudges. Thinks. is a private studio designed for solo or small-circle practice. You decide what’s shared and when.

How does the app adapt to me?

We analyze tone, pacing, biofeedback, and ritual completion with on-device intelligence. Insights stay encrypted and surface as gentle recommendations only you can see.

Can I import my existing practices?

Yes. Import meditations, breath exercises, or journal prompts and our ritual engine redesigns them with Thinks.’ color language and pacing.

What devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad at launch, with optional mirror display and Apple TV scenes for your meditation space. Mac and Vision Pro layouts arrive soon.
